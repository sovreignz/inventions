MindShot

Snapshot analog intelligence. Instantly vectorized. Locally owned.

Capture the Uncapturable

MindShot is a trigger-based vectorization tool for your whiteboards, journals, books, and even your voice. Point, shoot, and embed your analog ideas directly into your personal knowledge system.

Why MindShot?

  • No cloud. Local-first knowledge capture.
  • Bundled voice notes + snapshot images.
  • Perfect for teams, researchers, creatives, and sovereign builders.
  • Pairs seamlessly with your analog workflows.
